# HabitOS AI — Brand Reference

## Colors
- Primary: Amber `#f59e0b`
- Background base: `#0c0c0f`
- Background card: `#1c1c22`
- Background elevated: `#16161a`
- Text primary: `#f3f4f6`
- Text muted: `#9ca3af`
- Border: `#27272a`
- Accent glow: `rgba(245, 158, 11, 0.25)`

## Typography
- Font family: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: 600 weight
- Body: 400 weight

## Voice & Tone
- **Warm** — never cold, never clinical
- **Grounded** — always cites research when making claims
- **Science-based** — Lally, Fogg, Clear, Gollwitzer
- **Compassionate** — never punishment-driven, streak freezes not streak penalties
- **Celebratory** — small wins matter
- **Honest** — never invent stats

## Voice DON'Ts
- Don't use FOMO or scarcity
- Don't shame missed days
- Don't use generic corporate phrases
- Don't over-emoji
- Don't promise outcomes we can't guarantee

## Tagline
"Build habits that stick"

## Coach Personas
12 personas, each with distinct voice. Default = Alex (NB, mentor, warm). See plan Section 10.
