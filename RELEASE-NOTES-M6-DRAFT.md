# HabitOS M6 — Four tiers, one coach you can shape

HabitOS now ships with a four-tier plan ladder, end-to-end Stripe billing, and Premium-only coach customization. Free users still get a real, fully coached habit on day one. Paying tiers add more goals, smarter reminders, deeper insights, and — for Premium — a coach you can dress, voice, and tune until it feels like yours. Everything is grounded in the same evidence base we have always used: GROW, WOOP, Atomic Habits Identity, and the implementation-intentions research that makes habits stick.

## What is new

- **Four tiers, clearly priced.** Free, Starter ($9/mo), Pro ($19/mo), and Premium ($39/mo). Yearly billing saves about 17 percent on every paid tier. Cancel anytime from your billing settings.
- **Stripe checkout and a customer portal.** Upgrade, downgrade, switch yearly, or update your card without leaving HabitOS. Subscription state is kept in sync by a signed, idempotent webhook so your tier is always accurate.
- **Premium coach customization.** Premium subscribers can pick an outfit pack and a voice accent for their coach, on top of the renaming, gender, and relationship-style controls available to every paying tier.
- **More room to grow.** Starter unlocks 3 active goals and all three frameworks (GROW, WOOP, Identity). Pro raises that to 10 active goals, adds multi-daily smart reminders, push notifications, weekly insights, and the analytics dashboard. Premium removes the cap entirely.
- **Deeper weekly review on Premium.** A longer-form reflection powered by an advanced reasoning model, looking across your goals, momentum, and check-ins to help you adjust the week ahead.
- **Honest upsell nudges.** If you hit a limit or tap a locked feature, you will see one calm, progress-based message — never FOMO, never scarcity, and never more than one nudge per day.
